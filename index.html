About Nian Tech

Businesses and people deserve software that truly supports the way they work

The founders of Nian Tech are experienced software developers who have worked on both in-house and outsourcing projects across various industries. Throughout our journey, we've also been users and implementers of many enterprise software systems ourselves.

We noticed a common issue: business software systems are often just "good enough" — functional, but rarely truly well-fitted to the organization's real needs. This mismatch leads to wasted resources, operational bottlenecks, reduced performance, and sometimes even frustration for the people who rely on these systems every day.

At Nian Tech, we believe that every company — like every person — has its own unique needs. That's why we aim to be a trusted technology partner, working side by side with our clients to build the most fitting and effective solutions for each challenge.

Our Services

We provide flexible software development services designed to meet each client's unique needs. Whether you need a complete product, a dedicated team, or a long-term technology partner — we're here to help you build what truly fits.

Project-Based Development

We take full responsibility for delivering your software project from concept to completion. Our team works closely with you to define requirements, design the solution, and develop a product that aligns with your business goals.

Team-Based Development

We provide dedicated development teams that integrate seamlessly with your in-house staff. This model gives you the flexibility to scale your capacity while maintaining technical quality and consistent collaboration.

Blended Model

We start by developing your project, then continue to support and expand it through a dedicated team. This approach ensures long-term stability, knowledge continuity, and ongoing improvement after your product is launched.
