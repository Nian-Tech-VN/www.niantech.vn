About Nian Tech

The story behind our name and mission

The founders of Nian Tech are experienced software developers who have worked on both in-house and outsourcing projects across various industries. Throughout our journey, we've also been users and implementers of many enterprise software systems ourselves.

We noticed a common issue: business software systems are often just "good enough" — functional, but rarely truly well-fitted to the organization's real needs. This mismatch leads to wasted resources, operational bottlenecks, reduced performance, and sometimes even frustration for the people who rely on these systems every day.

At Nian Tech, we believe that every company — like every person — has its own unique needs. That's why we aim to be a trusted technology partner, working side by side with our clients to build the most fitting and effective solutions for each challenge.
